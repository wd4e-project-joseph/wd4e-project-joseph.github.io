Animals facts

Octopuses Have Three Hearts!

Two hearts pump blood to their gills, while the third heart pumps blood to the rest of the body. When they swim, the third heart actually stops beating!

Butterflies Taste with Their Feet!

Butterflies have taste sensors on their feet, allowing them to stand on a plant and taste it to see if it’s good for laying eggs.

Brain facts

Your Brain Has Around 86 Billion Neurons!

Your brain contains about 86 billion nerve cells (neurons), which help send and receive information at lightning speed.

Your Brain Can Store Over 2.5 Petabytes of Information!

That’s equivalent to three million hours of TV shows—more than you could ever watch in a lifetime!

Contries facts

France Has the Most Time Zones

France has the most time zones in the world—12, because of its overseas territories located across different oceans!

Brazil Is Home to the Amazon Rainforest

Brazil contains about 60% of the Amazon Rainforest, which is often referred to as the "lungs of the Earth" due to its critical role in absorbing carbon dioxide.

History facts

The Great Wall of China Is Not Visible from Space

Contrary to popular myth, the Great Wall of China is not visible from space without the aid of telescopes. It's too narrow and blends in with the natural surroundings.

The First Olympics Were Held in 776 BC in Ancient Greece

The first ancient Olympic Games took place in 776 BC in Olympia, Greece. Athletes competed in foot races, and the games were held to honor the Greek god Zeus.
